About ZENCON

Our Story

Founded in 2017 by a team of experienced and adept professionals, Zencon Infotech is a Business-IT consulting firm that seamlessly combines business expertise with technology to navigate complexities and provide comprehensive solutions. Our founders bring a wealth of domain expertise, having held instrumental positions in the manufacturing and life sciences industries, spanning critical supply chain functions such as planning, strategic sourcing, process engineering, and production. Additionally, they have extensive IT service and consulting experience from working with global brands in sectors like manufacturing, automotive, steel, life sciences, consumer packaged goods, retail, and utilities.

At Zencon, our philosophy revolves around creating value by harmonizing domain knowledge, enterprise technologies, and digital innovations to reimagine business possibilities. Since our inception, we have been steadfastly committed to transforming small and medium-sized companies by redefining ERP and delivering innovative, tailored, and streamlined solutions. Our approach has enabled our clients to become more efficient, simplify business operations, enhance supply chain visibility, make informed decisions, and optimize return on investment.

WHAT WE DO

At Zencon, we pride ourselves on our extensive experience in client collaboration, which has enabled us to proactively develop over 50 pre-configured bespoke solutions on SAP. These solutions facilitate faster implementation at reduced costs, ensuring accelerated value and quicker time-to-market. With more than 6 years of successful engagements, we have earned the trust and confidence of over 30 satisfied customers.

SAP S/4HANA is a game-changer, revolutionizing businesses across diverse industries by driving operational efficiency, innovation, and competitiveness in the digital era. Our comprehensive S/4HANA solutions, ranging from assessment and revival to seamless implementation, are tailored to optimize your operations for resounding success. Experience enhanced efficiency, visibility, and a competitive edge through business process automation, end-to-end integration, and the power of IIoT, QR Code, RFID, and Bar Code technologies.

With our reliable managed services and ongoing support, you can rest assured that your SAP environment will operate at peak performance. We recognize that every industry faces unique challenges, and our industry-specific insights and solutions have helped customers address these pain points effectively.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make a significant difference in the world by enabling enhanced efficiency and enriching lives.

Through our innovative solutions, we empower individuals, organizations, and institutions to drive economic growth, foster social advancement, and contribute to a sustainable environment.

We are dedicated to creating positive impacts, ensuring that people, businesses, and communities thrive and flourish, making the world a better place for all.

Our Mission

To be the beacon of digital transformation**, guiding small and medium businesses through the complexities of SAP solutions. We envision a future where our *expert SAP consulting* empowers companies to *unlock their full potential*, driving innovation and growth in an ever-evolving digital landscape. Our mission is to create *lasting value* for our clients and stakeholders, fostering a world where businesses of all sizes can harness the power of cutting- edge technology to thrive and succeed.
